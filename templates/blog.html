Hamish's Thoughts Part 2: Electric Boogaloo

25/08/2017 1:32 AM

Somtime's people ask me if I'm a lunatic or a genius. I'm always on the line - just like every great pioneer of their craft that was before me. Tesla was declared a mad scientist. Edison electrocuted elephants. Steve Jobs was a slave driver. Do be the best you have to do whatever it takes - and sometimes that means dedicating your mind and body 100% to your craft. All I know is that I'm the only one in the world making expertly crafted firewood and I know it's what I'll be known for when the dust settles and the history books are written

- Hamish
