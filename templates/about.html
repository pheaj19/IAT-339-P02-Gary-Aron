Hi my name is Hamish Dupree, and I am the founder and craftsman behind North America's only, premium, artisanally-crafted firewood. I grew up around wood growing up - wood burning stove, firewood, fireplaces. It's in my blood.

Started in 2010, Hamish and Co was established after I realizing that firewood has lost all of it's authenticity. My mission was to bring it back. That's why I only source the best, local firewood available and pour my heart and soul into each piece, while combining traditional and modern techniques. Every log tell's a story and it is my job to make it visible to you - no matter how long it takes.
