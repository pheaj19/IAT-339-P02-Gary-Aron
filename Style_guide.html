Brand Story

Hamish and Co. is an artisanal firewood workshop that specializes in crafting the best firewood on the market for burning or display
Sourced and crafted locally in Vancouver, BC, Hamish and Co. sells a variety of firewood collections and packages to those seeking
the most fragrant, strong burning firewood in the market, connoisseurs of craft, local business supporters, and appreciators of beauty.

Besides their product, Hamish and Co. also prides itself in educating and sharing what the artisanal movement is all about through events
and workshops hosted in both their storefront and workshop.

As a brand, Hamish and Co. represents community and authenticity, dedication, and craftsmanship behind their product. They allow the product to speak for itself.
